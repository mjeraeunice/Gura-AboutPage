OUR STORY

Gura was founded in 2010 by a group of fashion enthusiasts who wanted to create a brand that celebrated diversity and inclusivity.Our mission is to provide our customers with the latest fashion trends while promoting a positive and empowering message. We started small, designing and creating our own pieces in our living rooms and selling them at local markets. As our brand grew, we knew we wanted to stay true to our roots and continue to create clothing that was both stylish and sustainable. Today, we are proud to say that our brand is not only a reflection of our personal style, but also our commitment to ethical and sustainable fashion. We work with local artisans and use eco-friendly materials to create our pieces. We believe that fashion should not only look good, but also do good.
